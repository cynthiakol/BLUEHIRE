Privacy Policy

Learn how BlueHire protects and uses your data responsibly

Last Updated: October 2025

At BlueHire, we value your privacy and are committed to safeguarding your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect data when you use our platform or related services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal details such as your name, contact information, resume, job listings, and verification documents. These details help facilitate job matching and ensure safe interactions between employers and applicants.

2. Use of Information

We use collected data to:

  • Authenticate and verify users
  • Process job postings and applications
  • Improve BlueHire’s platform features and experience
  • Comply with academic research requirements as part of the capstone project

3. Data Sharing

BlueHire does not sell or rent your information. Data may only be shared with authorized academic supervisors or research partners for system evaluation purposes under confidentiality agreements.

4. Data Retention

Data collected is retained only for the duration of the academic project and securely deleted once the evaluation phase ends.

5. Your Rights

Users may request data updates or deletion by contacting the project team. Requests are handled according to ethical standards and project guidelines.

6. Security

We implement safety measures such as data encryption and access restrictions. However, as this is a student research platform, it does not operate as a commercial-grade service.

7. Policy Updates

We may revise this policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected on this page with the new effective date.

8. Contact Us

For questions about this Privacy Policy, reach out via our Help Center or project contact email.

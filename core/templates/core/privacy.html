Frequently Asked Questions

01 What is BlueHire?
BlueHire is a student capstone project that connects Filipino job seekers with employers in blue-collar industries. It's designed to demonstrate digital hiring solutions and does not facilitate actual employment transactions.
02 Is BlueHire free to use?
Yes. BlueHire is entirely free since it's for educational and research purposes only. No payments or commercial transactions are made through the system.
03 How do I post a job?
Employers can click "Post a Job" on the homepage or employer page. You'll be asked to sign in or create an account to continue.
04 Are applicants verified?
Yes. Applicants must upload barangay, police, or NBI clearance to confirm their identity before appearing in search results.
05 Who can I contact for help?
For any questions or technical concerns, please reach out through our contact form on the Contact Page or email our project support team.
